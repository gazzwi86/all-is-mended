About me

Hi, I'm Shelley, the editor behind All is mended.

If you can’t tell from my Shakespeare-inspired business name, I’m a bit of a word nerd, with a Bachelor of Arts (Literary Studies) and a Masters in Publishing and Communication. I'm also a member of the Institute of Professional Editors in Australia.

I've spent the last few years in sales and marketing, honing my ability to translate technical terms and corporate jargon into clear, concise language.

With ten years' experience as a freelance writer and editor, working for fintech companies, publishing houses, online businesses and not-for-profit organisations, I can do any odd job you require, whether you want to post online or print it oldschool, publish long think pieces, produce short product descriptions, or perfect your Instagram captions.
